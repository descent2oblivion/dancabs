About:

Daniel Ruvalcaba

Founder & Entrepreneur

I've been around turquoise my whole life, my grandfather started in the 90's and my father has kept it going until the present day. Over two decades of being in the turquoise business backs my expertise in turquoise cabs and rough. I do this job full time with a passion; I love cutting and cabbing amazing stones with turquoise mainly from the Southwestern U.S and bringing them to you!




Our mission

Provide a wide variety of affordable turquoise cabochons that any silversmith experienced or not can enjoy




Our vision

Every stone has its character, and our vision is to make sure every stone we make has a great story to tell!
